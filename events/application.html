HOW DOES THE APPLICATION PROCESS WORK?

APPLICATION PHASE

February 10, 2025 - March 20, 2025

EVALUATION PHASE

March 21-25, 2025

FINAL SELECTION

March 26, 2025

I, APPLICATION PHASE

Esports Clubs from around the world are welcome to apply for the 2025 Club Partner Program through the 10th of February 2025 to 20th of March 2025. The application takes about an hour to complete and asks for key information about their game statistics and general information.

II. EVALUATION PHASE

Applications will be reviewed by the APLK team in March 21-25, 2025. The evaluation process will be based on the information provided in the application form and the alignment of the applicant's vision with APLKâ€™s mission and values.

III. FINAL SELECTION

Selected students will be notified in March 26, 2025. The selected students will also be announced on the APLK's website.
