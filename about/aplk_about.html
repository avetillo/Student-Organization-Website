About Us

What is APLK (Apolaki)?

APLK stands for Apolaki, named after the powerful Filipino deity of war and the sun. In Philippine mythology, Apolaki is known as the patron of warriors and a symbol of strength, courage, and strategic brilliance.

Just as Apolaki commands the battlefield in mythology, our esports club aims to dominate the digital arena through skill, teamwork, and strategic excellence. We embody the warrior spirit in every game we play and every competition we enter.

Founded in 2025, APLK is more than just a gaming club – we are a community of dedicated students united by our passion for competitive gaming and esports culture. Our organization provides a platform for gamers to develop their skills, build meaningful connections, and represent our school in local and national tournaments.

Our Mission

At APLK, our mission is to foster a vibrant and inclusive gaming community that promotes sportsmanship, skill development, and camaraderie among student gamers. We aim to:

• Provide opportunities for students to showcase their gaming talents in competitive environments

• Create a supportive space where gamers can connect, learn, and grow together

• Organize tournaments and events that elevate the esports scene within our campus and beyond

• Promote responsible gaming practices and maintain a healthy balance between academics and gaming

Our Vision

We envision APLK becoming a recognized powerhouse in collegiate esports, known for developing top-tier talent, hosting premier gaming events, and creating an inclusive community where every member can thrive. We strive to break stereotypes surrounding gaming and establish esports as a legitimate and respected activity within our academic institution.
